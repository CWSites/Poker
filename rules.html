Rules

They're pretty simple

  1. You Do Not Talk About Poker.
  2. YOU DO NOT TALK ABOUT POKER!
  3. Someone Yells "STOP", Goes All-In Or Folds - Their Hand Is Over.
  4. Only Two Players To A Heads-up.
  5. One Hand At A Time.
  6. No Shirt, No Shoes - Except For Pics, Pics Are Public.
  7. Hands Will Go On As Long As The Timer Does.
  8. If This Is Your First Time Playing Poker, YOU HAVE TO PLAY.
If you want to actually read then go here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas_hold_'em#Rules
